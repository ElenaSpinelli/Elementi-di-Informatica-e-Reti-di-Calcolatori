ABOUT ME

Hello, I'm Elena Spinelli, a twenty-one-year-old Interior Design student at Politecnico di Milano, and this is my website.

In this website I'd like to show you my most important works in various connected fields, like interior design, graphics, painting and, in general, art.

I try to have a modern, wide, differentiated approach to art, taking inspiration from very different things (in short words, from anything that catches my eye). This is probably also an effect of my education, since I studied Classics at high school, while now I'm focusing on Design, thus having more than one way to understand and make art.

I'm curious and creative, open to a wide variety of life and artistic experiences, always in search of a way to improve myself and enrich my culture. I consider myself as a world citizen and I think that new generations should have a global, international view, taking the best of any culture which they have to deal with.
